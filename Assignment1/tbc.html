Tamil Baptist Church Villivakkam

situated at central part of Chennai city having a reformed faith, it has a strong congregation of about 300 families with 18 branch Churches. We preach Christ & the glorious message of the cross. We believe in the Sovereignty of God, Total Depravity of Man and God's rescue plan for man in Christ as the substitute for sinners who trust in Jesus. In Christ alone, by Faith alone.The Mission of Christ to spread the Gospel is our Vision.

What we do for the Youth?



Youth Meetings are for 25-35 participants (aged 12-13, 14-15, 16-18 or 19+), coordinated by adult staff. Young people aged under 16 travel in delegations with an adult leader.

What we do for Children?



Group VBS makes it easy for you to create your best VBS yet! All of our programs are field tested and designed with you in mind—we do the work, so you don’t have to!

What we do for Women?



We want to intentionally meet women who want deeper theological reflection where they are. It won’t just happen. Women’s ministry also allows us to directly address the challenges of cultivating friendships across generations—whether someone is mentoring or being poured into.

What we do for men?



Men want to be acknowledged. They want to believe that someone knows they exist and cares for them. They have a desire to be known.
